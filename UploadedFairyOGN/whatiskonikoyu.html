What Is Konikoyu

Kanikoyu is an acronym for the Japanese phrase, "Kankoku to Nihon no manga no yūgō". It combines the layout of Manga, the aesthetics of Josie manga and Seinin manwha. It focuses primarily on individual trans female characters in a dystopian setting.

Shikoma, or Four Frame Novels, will be the primary format of Konikoyu. Which themselves will evolve into the format of Besonasige, which combines aspects of Manga with American "Comics Poetry".

Comics Poetry is a format into itself that is worth studying to understand where I seek to take Konikoyu and Besonasige: Comics Poetry

Also worth researching is Text Comics, as I seek to revive the format of Text Comics for the modern age: Text Comics

Eventually I may go the route of marketing specifically to Korean immigrants to Japan, and other forms of Gaijin as well. And these can be considered of the "Gaijin Left"

Additionally I'm going to be gradually standardizing and reworking the Fraponic language, with Fraponic culture being similar to the culture of countries conquered by Japan and France in the Indochina wars.

Art style with blend elements of American comic strips and 4koma.
