GAME CONCEPT

PREMISE

A man born as a half-breed between a Loup-Garou and an Okuri-Inu, might battle between his darker nature and his desire to guide the dead. Here he comes to grips between the cultural differences between French-America and Japanese-America, as two sides compete over a great hunt against mankind. But when he falls in love with a woman that speaks a Rocky Mountain creole that blends Japanese and French, his loyality is split between the human's welfare and those who haunt the night. Also in his way are human experiments from an extension of Unit 731, merging with MKUltra. With human experimentations that want them dead.

GAMEPLAY

The game straddles the line between tactical JRPG and survival horror, with limited supplies gradually increasing the difficulty. Damage from enemies reduces as the werewolf curse slowly takes its course, but with cost being that one slowly loses the ability to control the character. There is a timer that must run out before the player is able to regain control. Different types of enemeies increase this bezerker mode by varying amounts. The game allows you to level up the weapons designed to be used as such, such as axes and swords for decapitations, flashlights for lighting up rooms and caves. You can use make weapons from things lying on the ground. Weapons are only usable as the bezerker mode slowly increases, with human form being an every man that can only hide or block pathways. With the assumption that the MCs wear French Sabots or Japanese Geta, these are examples of makeshift weapons, with various opprotunities to buy new wooden shoes / sandals.

Dungeons are generated not be randomness, but rather adapting the user's play style. For example, if it detects that your gravitating to certain areas that have only one entrance / exit, and using furniture to block the pathway to build a fort, it can adjust its strategy by varying the level of intelligence the enemy has, with smarter enemies relyong on social engineering, and this where Visual Novel gameplay elements are introduced. These paths depend on your understanding of the French and Japanese languages, as you'll meet characters over time with varying abilities to communicate in American English.

Here there a branching narrative that depends on what choices you make in choose your own adventure mode, and what degree of Bezerker mode you end on. The ending mainly focuses on the final fate or outcome of your gilrfriend that you're helping guide on the adventure. The worst ending with your girlfriend captured by each military and experimented on, and a secret ending ( the best one ) the MC survives the end and goes on to father Half-Japanese and half-french children. And set up possibility for a continuation. The medium ending, the girlfriend, after the MC is defeated in bezerker mode, is charged with aiding the enemy ( whether France or Japan depending on what region of the US you're in ) guillotined on either the German or French models.

AMBIANCE

Sounds effects blend elements from Gothic Fiction and Japanese mythology, with a focus on the faintest noise breaking the silence. This is where Iwato mode would be frequently used to set the tone.

MUSIC

Music is inspired by a mixture of Gothic Rock and Japanese Progressive Rock, with complex arrangements used to contrast haunting melodies and chord progressions.
